Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

1978
The First Saree

Ramanlal Shah opens a 200 sq.ft. shop in Kalupur with sarees sourced on a personal journey through India's weaving villages.

78
85
1985
South India Calls

The first journey to Kanchipuram establishes direct weaver relationships. Kanjivaram silks join the collection.

1994
The House is Born

Panetar moves into its iconic Silk Bazaar premises — a showroom with the space and grandeur the collection deserves.

94
02
2002
A New Generation

Rajiv Shah takes the helm, bringing a modern eye for curation while honouring every thread of his father's vision.

Today
The Journey Continues

Over 10,000 families served, 12 saree varieties, three generations — and a story still being written thread by thread.
